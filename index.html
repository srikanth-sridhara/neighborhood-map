    Features of MapSearch


    1] This is a neighborhood search app. With this, you will get a list of 15 places of various categories.
    2] The categories are: Restaurants, Parks, Cafe's and Gas Stations.
    3] You can filter the results by searching in the top bar or searching by category in the sidebar.
    4] There is also a map with markers to those places.
    5] Clicking on a marker or the list item will open an infoWindow with:
    > A street view > More information.
    6] In the More information section, data about the location is obtained from Yelp and Foursquare APIs.
    7] Clicking on the MapSearch logo will reset the map to initial default zoom value and center the map.
